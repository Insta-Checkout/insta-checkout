# Design System — Light Purple

*White background with purple accents. Same structure as Light Mode, but purple instead of orange for CTAs and decorative elements.*

---

## Target

**Insta Checkout** — Clean, bright, high-trust fintech aesthetic. White-dominant with purple as the primary accent (CTAs, icons, badges).

---

## Pattern

**Name:** Funnel (3-Step Conversion)

- Same section structure as Light Mode: Hero, Pain Points, How It Works, Features, Social Proof, Pricing, Final CTA
- Hero and Final CTA use **deep purple background** (`#2D0A4E`) with white text

---

## Style

- **Keywords:** Clean white, purple accents, modern, professional, trust-forward
- **Effects:** Subtle shadows, light purple tint sections, smooth transitions (200–300ms)
